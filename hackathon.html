Project Details

In this intense 24-hour coding competition with 15 participating teams, our team collaborated to build an interactive full-stack mapping application for the Northcoast Regional Land Trust to help visualize and manage conservation areas. Working around the clock, we demonstrated our ability to rapidly prototype, problem-solve under extreme time pressure, and deliver a functional solution that impressed the judges enough to earn us 2nd place out of all competing teams.

The hackathon challenged us to think creatively, work efficiently as a team, and implement both front-end and back-end technologies in a cohesive application. This experience showcased our technical skills, teamwork, and ability to deliver results in high-pressure environments.
